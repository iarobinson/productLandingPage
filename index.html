About

My first location independent gig (meaning the first time I made money online) was producing a podcast for Lewis Howes. Lewis introduced me to lots of his friends and before I knew it, I had produced hundreds of podcasts for a LOT of different people.

While producing all those shows myself, I developed a very efficient process for banging out good consistent podcasts. In this course, I go step-by-step through that process to teach you how to do what I learned over the course of those years as a podcast producer.

If you follow along with this video course, you will produce an actual podcast. We create recordings, engineer the recordings into .mp3 files, build a website which hosts the podcast and we then serve the podcasts to podcast networks. Indeed, you will see your name on the iTunes charts.
